MESSAGE FROM PRINCIPAL'S DESK

Gaining Knowledge alone is not Education, but it is that what makes us better human beings and so determines the future of the country. Education should help one to acquire life skills, there by live well, behave well to others and contribute his/her best to the society / nation / world. Ultimately the aim of Edcation should be the real peace in the world, where persons only use, develop and spread the positives in them.

We at KJSP, help in making this Pupil. Our goal a KJSP is to provide an enriching, engaging, and challenging curriculum that will prepare our Pupil Teachers for succes. Teachers here are trained to step au and use the latest technologes and Methodoligies for teching - learning process. Various activities, Workshops and Celebrations help them to be Sensitive and Empathetic towards the needs of the Young generation and Society at Large. We lay special emphasis on both co-curricular and extra-curricular activities providing our Pupil Teachers to be a leader in their respective field of acitivity.

Father of our nation, Mahatma Gandhi has beautifully observed the meaning of Education as "the all-round drawing out of the best in child and man, in body, mind ans spirit". Here in this College, we are in that effort to bring out the best in our student-teachers, in all aspects, so that they are equipped to do the same when they become the real teachers later.

We welcom all individuals who think they can contribute in the field of education as a teacher and mentor fot our young INDIA.

Mrs. Rupali M. Sadare

(B.Ed., M.A, M.Ed.)

Principal
