ABHAY SIKSHAN KENDRA

It was the year 1970 Abhay Shikshan Kendra was established in the form of social educational institue. Abhay Shikshan Kendra is a not-for-profit organization with an overarching goal to facilitate a just quitable, human and sustainable society with the vision. The foundation's work is aimed at enriching and enhansing the capabilities od individuals, group and institutions to achieve well being and harmony in a societ and culturally diverse democracy. For nearly a decade, the foundation has been egaged in contribution to quality in INDIA.

Abhay Shikshan Kendra was established in by Hon. Founder Shri. Eknathji M.Gaikwad (MP) with the sole motto of imparting education for the poorer, economical backward and social marfinalized section of the society. Inauguration of extended building of Abhay Shikshan Kendra was organized on 4th March,2003 with the auspicious hands of Hon. Sushilkumar Shinde, Former Chief Minister, and Maharashtra State in presence of Shri, Murli Deora.
